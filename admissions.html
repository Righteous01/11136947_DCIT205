Admission Requirements

To be considered for admission to the Computer Science program, applicants must meet the following requirements:

  • Completed application form
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or a related field
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0 on a 4.0 scale
  • Submission of official transcripts from all previous institutions
  • Letters of recommendation from academic or professional sources

Application Process

The application process involves the following steps:

  1. Fill out the online application form on our website.
  2. Submit official transcripts from all colleges or universities attended.
  3. Provide three letters of recommendation, preferably from academic or professional sources.
  4. Write a personal statement outlining your career goals and reasons for pursuing a graduate degree in Computer Science.
