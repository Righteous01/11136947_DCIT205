Admission

Undergraduate Programs

Explore our undergraduate programs in Computer Science and Information Technology. Learn from expert faculty, engage in cutting-edge research, and prepare for a successful career in the tech industry.

Admission Requirements:

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0
  • ACT/SAT scores
  • Letters of recommendation

Graduate Programs

Advance your knowledge with our graduate programs in Computer Science. Whether you're interested in artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, or software engineering, our programs provide a solid foundation for your career.

Admission Requirements:

  • Bachelor's degree in a related field
  • Minimum GPA of 3.5
  • GRE scores
  • Statement of purpose
